# NHL Back-to-Back Comprehensive Analysis (3 Seasons)

## Executive Summary

Analysis of **802 back-to-back situations** across 3 NHL seasons reveals a **consistent 5-6% home advantage** when teams play Game 2 of a back-to-back at home.

### **🎯 Key Finding:**
Teams playing **Game 2 at HOME** in back-to-back situations win **48.3%** of the time, compared to **42.6%** on the road.

**Home Advantage: +5.7 percentage points**

---

## Data Summary (3 Seasons)

### Season-by-Season Breakdown

| Season | Total B2Bs | Game 2 HOME Win% | Game 2 ROAD Win% | Home Advantage |
|--------|-----------|------------------|------------------|----------------|
| **2022-23** | 409 | 48.0% (59/123) | 42.3% (121/286) | **+5.7%** |
| **2023-24** | 359 | 49.2% (59/120) | 43.1% (103/239) | **+6.1%** |
| **2024-25*** | 34 | 75.0% (6/8) | 38.5% (10/26) | +36.5% |

*2024-25 is early season data (Oct 1-24) - small sample, likely variance

### **Combined 2022-24 (768 B2Bs):**
- **Game 2 at HOME**: 48.6% win rate (118/243 games)
- **Game 2 on ROAD**: 42.7% win rate (224/525 games)
- **Home Advantage**: **+5.9 percentage points**

## Travel Pattern Analysis

### Best to Worst Patterns (Combined 2022-24 Data)

| Pattern | Win Rate | Sample Size | Ranking |
|---------|----------|-------------|---------|
| **ROAD → HOME** | **49.2%** | 168 games | 🥇 Best |
| **HOME → HOME** | **48.0%** | 75 games | 🥈 |
| **HOME → ROAD** | **43.3%** | 164 games | 🥉 |
| **ROAD → ROAD** | **42.4%** | 361 games | ⚠️ Worst |

### Key Observations:

1. **ROAD → HOME is the best pattern** (49.2% win rate)
   - When teams travel for Game 1, then return home for Game 2
   - 168 games over 2 seasons

2. **ROAD → ROAD is the worst pattern** (42.4% win rate)
   - Teams staying on the road for both games
   - Largest sample size (361 games)

3. **Home advantage exists but is modest** (5-6%)
   - Not as large as the early 2024-25 data suggested
   - But consistent across 2 full seasons

## Betting Strategy

### ✅ **Recommended Approach**

**Primary Strategy:**
- **BET ON home teams** in Game 2 of back-to-backs
- Expected edge: **+5-6%**
- Historical win rate: **48-49%**

**Enhanced Strategy:**
- **Prioritize ROAD → HOME pattern** (49% win rate)
- **Avoid ROAD → ROAD teams** (42% win rate)

### 📊 **Expected Value Analysis**

At typical NHL odds (-110 both sides):
- Breakeven win rate: ~52.4%
- Our win rate: ~48-49%
- **Conclusion: No profitable edge at standard odds**

**However:**
- If you can find **home teams getting plus money** (e.g., +110 or better)
- Or if the home team is **undervalued by the market**
- The 5-6% edge could be exploitable

### 💡 **Practical Application**

**When to bet:**
1. Home team in Game 2 of B2B is getting **+105 odds or better**
2. ROAD → HOME pattern with home team getting **even money or better**
3. Combine with other factors:
   - Strong home team vs weak road team
   - Starting goalie vs backup goalie
   - Team trends (hot streak vs cold streak)

**When to avoid:**
1. Standard -110/-110 odds (no edge)
2. Favorites at -150 or worse (need >60% win rate to profit)
3. ROAD → ROAD teams without other compelling factors

## Comparison to Early 2024-25 Data

### Why the Early Season Showed +36% Home Advantage

The 2024-25 early season (Oct 1-24) showed:
- Game 2 HOME: 75% (6/8 games) ← **Small sample!**
- Game 2 ROAD: 38.5% (10/26 games)
- Home advantage: +36.5%

**Explanation:**
- Sample size was only **34 back-to-backs**
- Game 2 at HOME was only **8 games** (too small!)
- This was **statistical variance**, not a real edge

**The true edge (from 768 situations) is +5.9%, not +36.5%**

## Conclusion

### ✅ **What We Know:**

1. **Home teams in Game 2 of back-to-backs have a real edge** (+5-6%)
2. **ROAD → HOME is the best pattern** (49% win rate)
3. **ROAD → ROAD is the worst pattern** (42% win rate)
4. **The edge is modest** - not enough to bet blindly at standard odds
5. **The edge is consistent** - validated across 768 situations over 2 seasons

### ❌ **What Doesn't Work:**

1. Betting all home teams at standard -110 odds (no profit)
2. Betting home favorites at -150+ (edge disappears)
3. Assuming the early 2024-25 +36% advantage is real (it's variance)

### 💰 **How to Profit:**

1. **Find undervalued home teams** in Game 2 of B2Bs
2. **Target ROAD → HOME patterns** with plus money odds
3. **Combine with other edges** (team strength, goalie matchups, etc.)
4. **Use as a tiebreaker** when two bets are otherwise equal

### 📈 **Next Steps:**

1. **Track current season** back-to-backs for betting opportunities
2. **Identify teams** that consistently outperform in B2B situations
3. **Monitor odds movement** to find value on home B2B teams
4. **Incorporate goalie fatigue** - does the backup play Game 2?

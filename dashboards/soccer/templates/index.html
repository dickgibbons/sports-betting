About the stat columns: Numbers come from Daily Reports/soccer_team_complete_stats.csv (and L10/L5 variants when you change the period). Only teams in that file, under the same league name as the dashboard, get stats. Leagues missing from the CSV—or team names that do not match—show dashes. To backfill leagues, run python3 dashboards/soccer/collect_soccer_team_stats.py (API calls; may take a while).
